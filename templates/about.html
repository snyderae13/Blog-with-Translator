About Us


This blog is dedicated to all things style and fashion related. With a high focus on smaller, heritage insipired brands we try to bring the best information and insights into the world of high-quality clothing. One particular area of the heritage clothing world we at Stlye Blog like to focus on is the Japanese-American Reproduction genre. There are many reasons as to why: construction, pattern making, meticulous attention to detail, and the way many of these Japanese brands create more modern takes on a truly timeless clothing style.

Interested in Becoming a Writer at Style Blog?

We are always looking for new writers to share their insights and knowledge on the subject of high-quality heritage wear. However we also hold very high standard for those writers that we do accept. So please keep in mind that you will have to send a sample of your writing along with a reason stating as to why you want to join the Style Blog family of writers and contributors.
